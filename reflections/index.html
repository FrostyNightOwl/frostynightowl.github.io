01
Scripture · Science · Cosmology

The Quran & What the Universe Already Knew

A seventh-century desert. No telescopes. No microscopes. No oceanography. And yet the words spoken there describe what science would not glimpse for a thousand years. Ten verses, examined in light of the discoveries that followed. The expansion of the heavens. Embryonic morphology. The barrier between two seas. Iron forged in dying stars. The reflected light of the moon. And the rational conclusion this evidence asks of an honest mind.
